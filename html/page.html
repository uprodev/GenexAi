Revolutionizing Systematic Literature Reviews in MedTech and Pharma with AI

Published: 24 September, 2024

Author: Ernesto M. Nogueira

Systematic Literature Reviews (SLRs) serve as a backbone in medicine and pharma providing valuable insight from a compilation of multiple studies. This critical review offers a comprehensive and clear snapshot of evidence on a specific question, thus guiding strategic decisions, including product launches, and paving the way for regulatory approvals. However, the labor-intensive process, consuming on average 68 weeks and costing north of USD 120,000, becomes an obstacle for companies, due to the manual tasks they undertake as they read and categorize articles.

GenexAI's AI-Powered Solution

Harnessing artificial intelligence's transformative potential, GenexAI aims to streamline this vital, albeit strenuous, process. With the power to read over a thousand articles per hour and deliver tabulated and traceable results, GenexAI tackles the manual labor head-on. The extensively automated process cuts the time and cost of SLRs by 50% to 70%, an impressive efficiency gain in a sector where speed and precision are paramount.

Impact on Decision-Making

While efficiencies alone are noteworthy, the impact this technology has on decision-making and strategic moves is truly significant. With the reduction in the SLR timeline, companies have the freedom to make quicker, well-informed decisions, pivoting product launches or shifting strategies without the burdensome wait times of traditional SLR methods
